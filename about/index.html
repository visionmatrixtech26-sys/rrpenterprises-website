Our Story

Built on Trust & Hard Work

RRP Enterprises was founded with a single conviction — that Indian businesses deserve a logistics partner they can genuinely rely on. Starting with a lean operation out of Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh, we grew by earning the trust of one client at a time, building a reputation for punctuality, transparency, and care.

Today, we operate a network of 500+ empanelled vehicles spanning the length and breadth of India. Our hubs in Anantapur and Tadipatri sit strategically close to the major industrial corridors connecting Hyderabad and Bangalore — giving our clients a logistical edge that few providers can match in this region.

What started as a transport company has evolved into a full-spectrum supply chain partner — equipped with proprietary technology (RRP i-Drive and RRP-TMS), a diverse cargo portfolio, and a team that treats every consignment as a personal responsibility.
